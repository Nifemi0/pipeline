// Capabilities

Enterprise Outreach on Autopilot.

Apex replaces an entire sales development team with AI agents — no templates, no manual prospecting, no expensive tools.

🎯

Hyper-Specific Scoring

Every lead is scored across 4 dimensions: review profile, operational status, category fit, and location density. Only HOT leads get pitched — no spray and pray.

🧠

Gemini-Powered Writing

The Writer agent uses Google Gemini 2.5 Flash to generate unique, researched pitches for every lead. No templates, no fill-in-the-blank — each email is written from scratch.

⏱️

9 Daily Batch Sessions

The pipeline processes ~450 leads daily across 9 scheduled windows, with built-in rate limiting and timezone respect. Set it and forget it.

🌆

26-City Coverage

Active in 26 US markets including New York, Miami, Washington DC, San Jose, Charlotte, Portland, and more. Expandable to any US metro area.

📊

Live Analytics Dashboard

Full admin panel with lead tables, score distributions, pipeline analytics, inbox view, and agent controls. Real-time data, no refresh needed.

🔌

API-First Architecture

Every agent is an independent module with a shared database. Run agents individually, orchestrate the full pipeline, or integrate with external tools via REST API.

// Partner Integration

Google Gemini Challenge

The Writer agent is powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash — generating personalized, researched sales pitches for every lead with intelligent fallback chains.

✓ Google Gemini Vultr Featherless Kraken

How Gemini Powers the Writer

The Writer receives structured lead data (name, category, location, score) plus optional live website analysis. A carefully engineered prompt instructs Gemini to generate a 2–3 sentence pitch that references something specific about each business. No pricing mentioned. No generic fluff. Professional, direct, helpful.

Model: gemini-2.5-flash · Temperature: 0.7 · Max tokens: 350

Sample Output

"Noticed Kys Logistics is operating without a web presence — your customers are searching for freight partners online and landing on competitors. A simple one-page site showing your service areas and fleet would capture that traffic. I'll build you a demo for free within 24 hours — just say yes."

→ Generated by Gemini 2.5 Flash, zero editing
