Capabilities

Blue-Collar Focus

Targets plumbers, electricians, roofers, contractors — the businesses most likely to need a website.

Multi-Signal Scoring

Phone, address, email, Facebook, and website detection — each lead scored hot / warm / cold automatically.

Fully Autonomous

How To Use
01

Explore the Dashboard

Click "Dashboard" to see total leads, hot/warm/cold breakdown, and agent status.

02

Browse Leads

Search by city, category, or score. Click any lead to see its analysis and pitch history.

03

Run Agents

Run the Analyst to score pending leads, the Writer to generate pitches, or the full Pipeline.

04

Track Results

Analytics page shows score distribution, top cities, categories, and daily activity.
